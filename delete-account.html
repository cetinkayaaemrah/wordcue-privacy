Delete Your Wordcue Account

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Wordcue account and the personal data associated with it. Wordcue is developed by an individual developer, contactable at emrah.cetinkaya.dev@gmail.com.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to emrah.cetinkaya.dev@gmail.com from the email address registered to your Wordcue account.
  2. Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  3. We will verify the request comes from the account owner and process it within 30 days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

Deleting the Wordcue app from your device does not delete your account or data — you must submit a deletion request as described above.
